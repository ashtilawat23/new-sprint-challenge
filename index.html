Challenge 1: Test Results

See the test results for Challenge 1 by opening your browser's console. Here's how:

  • Google Chrome: Press Ctrl + Shift + J on Windows/Linux or Cmd + Option + J on macOS.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Press Ctrl + Shift + K on Windows/Linux or Cmd + Option + K on macOS.
  • Safari: First, enable the Develop menu by going to Safari > Preferences > Advanced and checking "Show Develop menu in menu bar". Then, press Cmd + Option + C.
  • Microsoft Edge: Press Ctrl + Shift + I on Windows or Cmd + Option + I on macOS.
